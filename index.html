PixelPeg — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-05-13

1. Data Collection

PixelPeg does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app does not require account registration or login.

2. Local Storage

Game progress (cleared levels, settings) is stored locally on your device only and is never sent to any server.

3. In-App Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not collect or store any payment information.

4. Third-Party Services

We use RevenueCat solely to manage in-app purchase entitlements. Please refer to RevenueCat's Privacy Policy for details.

5. Children

PixelPeg is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Contact

If you have any questions, please contact us at: pixelpeg.game@gmail.com
