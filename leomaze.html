LeoMaze — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-14

1. Data Collection

LeoMaze collects the following data solely for the purpose of operating the global leaderboard feature:

  • Display name (nickname entered by the player)
  • Game scores and completion times
  • A randomly generated device identifier (UUID, not linked to any personal identity)

No name, email address, or other personally identifiable information is required or collected.

2. Local Storage

Game progress (cleared levels, settings, unlocked content) is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any server, except for leaderboard scores as described above.

3. Leaderboard

Leaderboard data (nickname, score, completion time, and device UUID) is stored on servers provided by Cloudflare, Inc. This data is used only to display global rankings within the app. Please refer to Cloudflare's Privacy Policy for details.

4. In-App Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by the Apple App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information. Purchase entitlements are managed via RevenueCat. Please refer to RevenueCat's Privacy Policy for details.

5. Children

LeoMaze is rated 9+ and is suitable for children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The only data stored remotely is the player's chosen nickname and game scores.

6. Data Deletion

Players may request deletion of their leaderboard data (nickname and scores) by contacting us at the email below.

7. Contact

If you have any questions, please contact us at: pixelpeg.game@gmail.com

隱私權政策（繁體中文）

最後更新：2026-06-14

1. 資料收集

LeoMaze 僅為提供全球排行榜功能而收集以下資料：

  • 顯示名稱（玩家自行輸入的暱稱）
  • 遊戲分數與過關時間
  • 隨機產生的裝置識別碼（UUID，不與任何個人身份連結）

本應用程式不要求也不收集姓名、電子郵件或其他可識別個人身份的資訊。

2. 本地儲存

遊戲進度（通關紀錄、設定、已解鎖內容）僅儲存於您的裝置本地，除排行榜分數外，不會傳送至任何伺服器。

3. 排行榜

排行榜資料（暱稱、分數、過關時間、裝置 UUID）儲存於 Cloudflare, Inc. 提供的伺服器，僅用於在應用程式內顯示全球排名。詳情請參閱 Cloudflare 隱私權政策。

4. 應用程式內購

內購項目由 Apple App Store 處理，我們不收集或儲存任何付款資訊。內購授權透過 RevenueCat 管理，詳情請參閱 RevenueCat 隱私權政策。

5. 兒童隱私

LeoMaze 年齡分級為 9+，適合兒童使用。我們不會主動收集兒童的個人資料。遠端儲存的資料僅限玩家自選暱稱與遊戲分數。

6. 資料刪除

玩家可透過下方聯絡信箱申請刪除其排行榜資料（暱稱與分數）。

7. 聯絡我們

如有任何疑問，請聯絡：pixelpeg.game@gmail.com
